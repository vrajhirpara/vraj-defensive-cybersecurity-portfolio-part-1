\# Activity A15 – Discover 5 Recent Security Incidents



\## 1. Bybit Cryptocurrency Theft (February 2025)

A major cryptocurrency theft resulted in $1.47 billion in Ethereum being stolen. This showed the importance of strong wallet security and approval controls.



Source: https://www.infosecurityeurope.com/en-gb/blog/threat-vectors/top-five-cyber-attacks-2025.html



\---



\## 2. Marks \& Spencer, Co-op, and Harrods Attacks (April–May 2025)

These attacks involved social engineering and ransomware, causing major financial losses and customer data exposure. They highlighted the importance of identity verification and staff awareness.



Source: https://www.cm-alliance.com/cybersecurity-blog/biggest-cyber-attacks-of-2025-their-impact-on-global-cybersecurity



\---



\## 3. Kettering Health Ransomware Attack (June 2025)

This ransomware attack disrupted hospital systems, phone lines, and medical services. It showed why healthcare organisations need segmented systems and reliable offline backups.



Source: https://www.csis.org/programs/strategic-technologies-program/significant-cyber-incidents



\---



\## 4. Asahi Breweries Ransomware Attack (September 2025)

This incident affected 1.9 million customers and disrupted business operations in Japan. It demonstrated the need for strong cybersecurity planning across large organisations.



Source: https://www.infosecurity-magazine.com/news-features/top-10-cyberattacks-of-2025/



\---



\## 5. Oracle EBS Zero-Day Exploit (October 2025)

The Clop group exploited an unpatched Oracle EBS vulnerability and used stolen data for extortion. This incident highlighted the importance of rapid patching of critical enterprise systems.



Source: https://www.infosecurityeurope.com/en-gb/blog/threat-vectors/top-five-cyber-attacks-2025.html
